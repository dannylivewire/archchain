Easy to Use & Customize

The idea behind this, is to make it as easy to use as possible. In the future we will add additional features to allow for custom data storage based on the user's needs.

Use Cases

eGovernance

Provide a papertrail from Government Bid and Contract Purchase Orders, to individual line items and reciepts that get uploaded to the Archchain network.

Secure Contracts

Archive and verify a contract on the public Etherum Swarm Blockchain through a URL or Unique ID and a Hash of the data for verification.

Stewardship

Responsible Governments, Corporations and Organizations can leverage this tool to show transparency by auditing their expenses and vendors.
